## 🚀 About Me

I'm a passionate and results-oriented **Cybersecurity Professional** with a deep-seated curiosity for the intricate dance between digital offense and defense. My journey in this field is fueled by the constant evolution of threats and the challenge of architecting resilient systems.

*   🔴 **Offensive Mindset (Red Team):** I thrive on dissecting systems, uncovering vulnerabilities, and simulating real-world attacks. My experience includes penetration testing, vulnerability research, exploit development, and red team operations, always aiming to identify weaknesses before malicious actors do.
*   🔵 **Defensive Fortitude (Blue Team):** Protecting assets is paramount. I focus on robust security architectures, threat intelligence, incident response, digital forensics, and proactive hardening of systems and networks to ensure operational integrity and data confidentiality.

My philosophy is rooted in **continuous learning and adaptation**. The cybersecurity landscape is ever-changing, and I am committed to staying at the forefront of new TTPs (Tactics, Techniques, and Procedures), tools, and defensive strategies.
