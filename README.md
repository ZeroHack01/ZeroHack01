## 🧭 My Compass: About Me

As a dedicated **Cybersecurity Professional**, I am driven by an insatiable curiosity to understand the intricate mechanics of the digital world – both its vulnerabilities and its defenses. My journey is one of perpetual learning, where the ever-shifting sands of cyber threats present constant challenges and opportunities for growth.

*   🔴 **The Art of Offensive Security (Red Team):** I specialize in emulating adversarial tactics to proactively identify and exploit weaknesses. This involves deep-dive vulnerability research, sophisticated penetration testing, and leading red team engagements to bolster an organization's resilience from the inside out.
*   🔵 **The Science of Defensive Security (Blue Team):** My focus here is on architecting robust security postures, leveraging advanced threat intelligence, orchestrating swift incident responses, conducting meticulous digital forensics, and instilling a culture of security awareness to safeguard critical assets.

I believe that true security lies in a holistic understanding – a balanced perspective that appreciates the attacker's ingenuity as much as the defender's diligence. This dual-faceted approach guides my strategies and my commitment to advancing the field.
